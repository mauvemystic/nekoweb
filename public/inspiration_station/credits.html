my inspiration station

My curated space for inspiration in good times and bad times alike. This includes tidbits I've picked up around the web and meaningful messages I've received that mean something to me. I made a similar page using Notion (which I use for other purposes), but there are many creative limits, leading to the making of this new home.

Note: Some photos or clippings are from unknown authors/artists; please let me know if you created something here so I can give you credit!

If you have Javascript enabled and are using a larger screen (computer or tablet), you can click the handles in the top right corner of each note and drag them around. This serves no real purpose, but it's fun.
