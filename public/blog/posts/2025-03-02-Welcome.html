First blog post, let's go!

I don't share much on social media to begin with. The only other site I'm actively using (Tumblr) is my place to shit-post and "micro-blog," but my intention here is to share not only my going-ons but my more thoughtful musings.

Will anyone read these posts? I don't know, and the goal is to not care about that.

So much of what is shared online these days is meant to answer the question of "how many views will I get," and I'm not good at playing that game. So I won't.
