Insight into this period of change/release:

Mystical Power

Mystical alignment. Self-acceptance. Technician of the sacred. Direct connection with Source. Emptiness. Number 7.

Server of Cups

Responding to the emotional and interpersonal needs of others in a ministering way; much of this happens through small acts. The best way to serve is as a channel for larger energy, to avoid personal energy depletion and/or distortion.

To stay healthy, you must keep in touch with your own needs, ministering to them as tenderly as you minister the needs of others. Giving out of fullness, not with the intent to receive in return.

Can you enjoy being compassionate without making it your identity? Do you get engulfed with others' needs?

The Ram & Dahlia

Your stubbornness is hindering your progress. While "ramming through" may seem like the best and quickest way to achieve success, some endeavors require flexibility, regular, assessment, or help from others.

*Aries archetype.

Conclusion

Acceptance of who I am and of my circumstances, loving myself and believing in myself regardless of these circumstances. Staying aware of the balance between tending to myself and tending to others. Releasing stubbornness, no longer trying to force a square through a circle hole. Getting creative with help from others, being flexible about the journey not looking like I expected it to.
