About this blog...

I've used tumblr for the majority of my "blogging" purposes in the past decade or so. I did spend time customizing my original account, but after deleting that account for multiple reasons, I prefer to use tumblr for shitposting or what the kids call microblogging.

My intention here is to share my more thought-out ideas or trains of thought to reflect on in the future.
